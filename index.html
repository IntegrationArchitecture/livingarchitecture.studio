Approach

Architecture at the scale of light.

Each artifact is treated as a small architectural system: structure, optics, electronics, and behavior tuned together to create a specific emotional response in the room. The work sits between art, product, and environmental interface.

Material integration
Wood, glass, diffusion media, and hardware are composed as a single system, not decoration on top of a form.
Structure Joinery
Light as behavior
Light is treated as a behavioral layer—slow, ambient, and responsive rather than performative or screen‑like.
Ambient Low frequency
Presence over spectacle
The goal is a quiet, persistent presence that makes a room feel more alive, not a gadget that demands attention.
Coastal minimal Long‑term
